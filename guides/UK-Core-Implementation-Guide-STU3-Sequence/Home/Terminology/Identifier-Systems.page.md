## {{page-title}}

<div markdown="span" class="alert alert-warning" role="alert"><h4><i class="fa fa-info-circle"></i> Identifier Systems Usage</h4>
Whilst every effort has been made to ensure that the examples are correct and useful, they are not a normative part of the specification.
</div>

In FHIR, an identifier system is a namespace (usually a URI or OID) that defines the scope and authority of an identifier — it tells you where the identifier comes from and how it should be interpreted.

<p>
  The <strong>NamingSystem</strong> resource can be accessed at <a href="https://hl7.org/fhir/R4/namingsystem.html" target="_blank">HL7 R4 NamingSystem</a>.
</p>
<p>
  For guidance on migrating OIDs to FHIR-compliant identifiers, refer to the 
  <a href="https://confluence.hl7.org/spaces/FHIR/pages/35718620/Migrating+OIDs+to+FHIR" target="_blank">
    HL7 Confluence page on Migrating OIDs to FHIR</a>.
</p>
